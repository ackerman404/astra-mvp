1. WHAT IS ASTRA?
================================================================================

Astra Interview Copilot is a desktop application that helps you during
interviews by:

  - Listening to your system audio in real time
  - Automatically detecting interview questions
  - Generating answers based on YOUR resume and documents
  - Displaying answers in two formats:
      * Quick bullet points (key talking points)
      * Conversational script (full response you can speak naturally)

Your documents never leave your computer. Only the interview questions are
sent to the AI service to generate answers.


================================================================================
2. SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

  - Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
  - Internet connection (required for AI answer generation)
  - Audio output device (speakers or headphones) for system audio capture
  - Your resume and relevant documents in .pdf, .txt, or .md format


================================================================================
3. INSTALLATION
================================================================================

  1. Run AstraSetup.exe
  2. Follow the installer prompts (Next > Next > Install > Finish)
  3. No admin rights are required - Astra installs in your user folder
  4. A desktop shortcut and Start Menu entry will be created

To uninstall: Use "Add or Remove Programs" in Windows Settings.
Your documents and settings are preserved after uninstall.

4. GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

Quick Start (3 steps):

  Step 1:  Launch Astra and activate your license key
  Step 2:  Ingest your documents (resume, job descriptions, etc.)
  Step 3:  Start a session and join your interview


================================================================================
5. LICENSE ACTIVATION
================================================================================

When you first launch Astra, you will see the License Activation screen.

  1. Copy your license key from the purchase email
  2. Paste it into the text field (Ctrl+V)
  3. Click "Activate License"

  - Green message  = Success! You're good to go.
  - Red message    = Invalid key. Double-check and try again.
  - Yellow message = Server offline. Key is saved and will validate later.

You can also click "Skip for now" to ingest documents without a license,
but you will need a valid license to use the interview session features.

Each license key is tied to one computer at a time. To use Astra on a
different machine, deactivate the license first (see Section 11).


================================================================================
6. INGESTING YOUR DOCUMENTS
================================================================================

Before your first interview session, you need to feed Astra your documents
so it can generate personalized answers.

  1. From the startup screen, click "Ingest Documents"
  2. Select a folder containing your files
  3. Wait for the progress bar to complete

SUPPORTED FILE TYPES:
  - .pdf  (resumes, job descriptions, certifications)
  - .txt  (plain text notes, project summaries)
  - .md   (markdown files)

TIPS:
  - Put all your documents in one folder for easy ingestion
  - Include your resume, project descriptions, and role-specific notes
  - You can re-ingest anytime to add new documents
  - Large PDFs are processed page by page (you'll see progress)

WHAT TO INCLUDE:
  - Your resume / CV
  - Job description for the role you're interviewing for
  - Project summaries or STAR-format stories you've prepared
  - Technical notes relevant to the position


================================================================================
7. STARTING AN INTERVIEW SESSION
================================================================================

  1. From the startup screen, click "Start Session"
  2. The main Astra window will open with a compact toolbar at the top
  3. Make sure your interview audio is playing through your computer
     (e.g., Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, phone call on speaker)
  4. Click the "Listen" button to start capturing audio
  5. Astra will automatically detect questions and generate answers

IMPORTANT: Astra captures your SYSTEM AUDIO (what comes out of your
speakers/headphones). Your interview call must be playing through your
computer for Astra to hear the interviewer's questions.


================================================================================
8. USING AUTO-ANSWER MODE
================================================================================

Auto-answer mode is enabled by default. Here's how it works:

  1. Click "Listen" to start
  2. Astra continuously monitors your system audio
  3. When someone stops speaking (silence detected), Astra transcribes
     what was said
  4. If it detects an interview question, it automatically generates
     an answer
  5. The answer appears in two panels:
       - Left side:  Quick bullet points (glance-friendly)
       - Right side: Full conversational script

STATE INDICATORS (top-left of toolbar):
  - White circle  = Ready / Idle
  - Blue circle   = Listening (waiting for speech)
  - Green circle  = Hearing speech
  - Yellow circle = Processing (transcribing & classifying)
  - Purple circle = Generating answer

The Q&A History panel on the left keeps all previous questions and answers
from your session. Click any history card to review past answers.


================================================================================
9. USING MANUAL MODE
================================================================================

If you prefer to control when Astra generates answers:

  1. Open Settings (gear icon in toolbar)
  2. Uncheck "Auto-answer"
  3. Click "Listen" to start audio capture
  4. When you want an answer, click the "Answer" button
  5. Astra will transcribe the last few seconds of audio and generate
     an answer

This is useful when you want to be selective about which questions
Astra responds to.


================================================================================
10. SETTINGS & CONTROLS
================================================================================

Click the gear icon in the toolbar to expand the settings panel.

AUDIO DEVICE
  Select which audio output device to capture from. Use the "Test"
  button to verify Astra can hear your audio.

JOB CONTEXT
  Enter the job title you're interviewing for (e.g., "Senior SAP MM
  Consultant"). This helps Astra tailor answers to the role.

AUTO-ANSWER
  Toggle automatic question detection and answer generation on/off.

CONFIDENCE SLIDER
  Controls how confident Astra needs to be that something is a question
  before generating an answer.
    - Lower value = More sensitive (answers more often, may respond to
      non-questions)
    - Higher value = More selective (only answers clear questions)
    - Default is usually fine for most interviews

TONE
  Choose the conversational tone for the script answer (e.g., professional,
  friendly, confident).


================================================================================
11. DEACTIVATING YOUR LICENSE
================================================================================

To transfer your license to a different computer:

  1. Open Settings (gear icon in toolbar)
  2. Scroll to the right end of the settings panel
  3. Click "Deactivate License"
  4. Confirm the deactivation

After deactivating, you can activate the same license key on another machine.

12. TROUBLESHOOTING & COMMON ERRORS
================================================================================

PROBLEM: "No audio monitor devices found"
  - Make sure your audio drivers are installed
  - On Windows, check that your speakers/headphones appear in Sound Settings
  - Restart Astra after plugging in a new audio device

PROBLEM: Astra is not hearing the interview audio
  - Your interview call must play through your computer speakers
  - Check that the correct audio device is selected in Settings
  - Use the "Test" button to verify audio capture is working
  - Make sure your interview app (Zoom/Teams/Meet) audio output matches
    the device selected in Astra

PROBLEM: "License key is not valid"
  - Double-check that you copied the full key with no extra spaces
  - Make sure you're using the key from your purchase email
  - If the issue persists, contact support (see below)

PROBLEM: "Key is active on another machine"
  - Your license is currently activated on a different computer
  - Deactivate it on the other machine first, then try again
  - If you no longer have access to that machine, contact support

PROBLEM: "Connection error" or "Server offline" during activation
  - Check your internet connection
  - The server may be temporarily down. Your key is saved locally and
    will validate automatically when the server is back online.

PROBLEM: Answers are slow or not appearing
  - Check your internet connection (answers require an online connection)
  - The AI service may be under heavy load. Wait a moment and try again.
  - If answers consistently fail, check that your license is active

PROBLEM: "Service is busy. Please wait."
  - You've hit the rate limit. Wait a few seconds before the next question.
  - This protects the service from overload and resets automatically.

PROBLEM: Ingestion crashes or fails
  - Try ingesting fewer files at once
  - Very large PDFs (100+ pages) may take longer to process
  - If a specific file fails, try converting it to .txt format
  - Check that your files are not corrupted or password-protected

PROBLEM: Astra doesn't detect questions correctly
  - Adjust the Confidence slider in Settings:
      Lower = detects more (but may false-trigger)
      Higher = more selective
  - Make sure the audio quality is clear (reduce background noise)
  - Astra works best with clear English speech

PROBLEM: App won't start or crashes on launch
  - Try running as administrator (right-click > Run as administrator)
  - Reinstall Astra using the installer
  - Make sure Windows is up to date
